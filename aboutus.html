About Supernetworks

Our Mission

At Supernetworks we're crafting a new standard for networking. Join us in our quest to make networking in the digital world safer, faster, and more transparent for everyone.

Our products empower you with superior network visibility and control. We're giving you the tools to understand and design your digital networks without having to give up security for convenience.

Our Founders

Our common values around empowering people come from our early days online, where we first met playing CTF contests at wargames.unix.se and Pull The Plug. We believe that learning is the best kind of fun, and that technology should supercharge people's abilities to make their lives better.

Alex Radocea

Mission Focus

"I felt like the routers I was buying were limiting my ability to manage and trust my home network.

I was looking to set up the fanciest networking gear on the market and I was surprised about how difficult it was to build secure networks to withstand expert attacks and block breaches.

This led us to innovate and design a better way to build a secure network while maintaining full device compatibility".

Founder's Background

In college, Alex Radocea was the inaugural President of RPISEC.

He was an intern with Matasano Security on Wall Street, on Apple's Product Security Team, an early employee at Crowdstrike building the first ever Mac EDR capable sensor & all things Apple malware intel. In Sweden, Alex worked on Spotify's magneto project and later helped build and guide all things security through hypergrowth to their NYSE debut.

Before founding Supernetworks with Philip, Alex started Longterm Security to help startups perform security architecture & software security testing.

Philip Olausson

Mission Focus
